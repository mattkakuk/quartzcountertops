## **What Are Quartz Countertops? An Introduction**

Quartz countertops are engineered surfaces created by blending natural quartz
crystals with high-quality resins and pigments. This process makes them not
just beautiful but incredibly resilient, ideal for high-traffic kitchens and
busy households.

### **The Composition of Quartz Countertops**

  * Natural quartz: 90-95%
  * Resin binder: 5-10%
  * Pigments and additives: for color and texture

This innovative composition results in a surface that mimics natural stone but
surpasses it in strength and ease of maintenance.
