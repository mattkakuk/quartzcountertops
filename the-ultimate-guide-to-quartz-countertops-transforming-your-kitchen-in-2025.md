## **What Are Quartz Countertops? An Overview**

### **Composition and Manufacturing**

Quartz countertops are engineered surfaces made from natural quartz crystals
combined with resin binders. This blend yields a highly durable, low-
maintenance material that mimics the beauty of natural stone.

### **The Benefits of Choosing Quartz**

  * **Durability:** Resistant to scratches, stains, and heat
  * **Low Maintenance:** Easy to clean with just soap and water
  * **Aesthetic Versatility:** Available in countless colors and finishes
  * **Hygienic Surface:** Non-porous, preventing bacterial growth
