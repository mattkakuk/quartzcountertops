## **Technical Essentials That Ensure Longevity**

### **Thickness: 3cm vs 2cm**

  * **3cm (~1-1/4 ″)** is Florida’s go-to—sturdy with minimal additional support.

  * **2cm (~3/4 ″)** works for European looks or shelves; may need build-ups or brackets.

### **Overhang & Support**

  * **Standard:** 1 ″–1.5″ overhang for perimeter runs.

  * **Seating Overhangs ( >10″):** Plan for hidden brackets or corbels placed to protect knees and the slab.

### **Seam Planning**

We minimize seams and place them where patterns align naturally—avoiding sink
centers when possible. Consistent quartz patterns make seams nearly invisible.

### **Cutouts & Clearances**

Manufacturer templates guide sink and cooktop openings; we leave thermal
expansion room for ranges and dishwashers, and finish edges cleanly.

## **Care & Maintenance: Easy Habits, Gorgeous Results**

### **Daily Cleaning**

Warm water, mild dish soap, soft cloth. For sticky spots, soak briefly and use
a non-abrasive pad.

### **Heat & Cutting**

Quartz tolerates brief warmth, but **use trivets** under hot pans and small
appliances. Always **use cutting boards** —protects your knives and your
finish.

### **What to Avoid**

Abrasives, paint strippers, drain openers, or harsh solvents. If something
aggressive spills, rinse immediately.

### **Stain Reality Check**

Quartz resists stains exceptionally well. Wipe turmeric, wine, or berries
promptly—especially on brilliant whites.

## **Outdoor & Sun-Drenched Spaces: A Quick Word on UV**

Not every quartz line is UV-stable. For outdoor kitchens or sun-soaked Florida
rooms, we’ll recommend materials designed for exposure—or plan shading—so your
color stays true.

## **FAQs**

### **1) How long does the process take from selection to installation?**

After you select your slab and we complete digital templating, fabrication
typically takes about a week. Most installations finish in a single day.
Complex waterfalls or full-height quartz backsplashes can extend
timelines—we’ll outline that upfront.

### **2) Will I see seams in my**[ **quartz
countertops**](https://www.google.com/maps?cid=2937615344441069978) **?**

We minimize seams and place them strategically where patterns align. With many
quartz designs, seams are subtle; with dramatic veining, we align the grain
for the cleanest visual flow and review locations with you before cutting.

### **3) Can I put hot pots directly on quartz?**

Use trivets or hot pads. Quartz handles brief warmth, but prolonged or extreme
heat can stress resin binders and dull the finish. Trivets protect both form
and function.

### **4) What edge is best for families with kids?**

Eased and half-bullnose edges are excellent—comfortable, low-profile, and easy
to wipe. They also soften accidental bumps around busy islands.

### **5) Do quartz countertops need sealing or special cleaners?**

No sealing required. Quartz is non-porous and highly stain-resistant. Use mild
dish soap and warm water for daily cleaning and avoid harsh solvents or
abrasives to keep surfaces bright.
