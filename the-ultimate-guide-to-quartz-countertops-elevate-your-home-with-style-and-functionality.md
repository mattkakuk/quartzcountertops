# What Are Quartz Countertops?

## Defining Quartz Countertops

Quartz countertops are engineered surfaces made from crushed natural quartz
crystals mixed with resins, pigments, and other materials. Together, these
components create a surface that mimics the appearance of natural stone but
offers superior strength and consistency.

### How Are Quartz Countertops Made?

Unlike natural stone, which is quarried from Earth’s crust, quartz countertops
undergo a manufacturing process where raw quartz material is combined with
polymers. This process results in a highly durable and customizable surface.

## The Rise of Quartz in Home Design

As homeowners seek options that are both stylish and practical, quartz
countertops have surged in popularity. They deliver the aesthetic appeal of
natural stone with added benefits like low maintenance and resistance to
stains.
