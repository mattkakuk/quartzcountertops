## **What Are Quartz Countertops?**

### **Defining Quartz Countertops**

Quartz countertops are engineered surfaces consisting of crushed natural
quartz, resin, and pigments. Unlike natural stone options like granite or
marble, quartz is manufactured to deliver consistent patterns, vibrant colors,
and superior performance.

**Why choose quartz?** Because it combines the natural aesthetics of stone
with the strength and low-maintenance qualities of engineered material—making
it ideal for busy kitchens and bathrooms.

* * *

## **Why Choose Quartz Countertops?**

### **Benefits Over Other Surfaces**

When selecting a countertop material, durability, appearance, and price are
key factors. Quartz countertops excel in all areas:

  * **Exceptional Durability:** Resistant to scratches, chips, and stains—perfect for high-traffic kitchens.
  * **Low Maintenance:** No sealing needed, unlike granite or marble.
  * **Design Versatility:** Available in a myriad of colors and patterns.
  * **Hygienic Surface:** Non-porous, preventing bacteria and mold buildup.
  * **Consistency:** Uniform look with no unpredictable veining or color patches.
