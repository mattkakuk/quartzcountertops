## **What Makes Quartz a Top Choice in Pompano Beach?**

Quartz isn’t just a pretty surface—it’s engineered for performance. It resists

### **Quartz vs. Natural Stone: What’s the Real Difference?**

Natural stone is beautiful, but it can be porous and high-maintenance. Quartz
is engineered from natural quartz particles combined with resins and pigments,
creating a durable surface that’s easier to care for—especially in humid
Florida conditions.

* * *

#### **Why Florida Homes Benefit From Non-Porous Surfaces**

In a coastal climate, moisture and humidity are constant. A non-porous surface
helps reduce staining, makes cleanup easier, and keeps your counters looking
fresh for years.

## **Quartz Care Tips: Keep It Looking New**

Quartz is low-maintenance, but a few habits help it stay perfect.

* * *

### **Daily Care**

  * Mild soap and water
  * Soft cloth or non-abrasive sponge
  * Wipe spills promptly (especially coffee/wine)

* * *

#### **What to Avoid**

  * Harsh chemicals like bleach-based cleaners (occasional use is usually okay, but don’t make it your default)
  * Abrasive pads
  * Direct heat from pans
