## **FAQs**

### **1) What are the best kitchen countertops for a busy household in Pompano
Beach?**

In our experience, durable options like quartz are ideal because they handle
spills, stains, and everyday use while staying easy to clean—perfect for
active coastal living.

### **2) Are white quartz countertops hard to keep clean?**

Not at all. With simple daily cleaning, **white quartz countertops** stay
bright and polished. We recommend mild soap and water, and wiping spills
quickly.

### **3) Do quartz countertops need sealing like granite?**

Quartz typically does not require sealing, which is a big reason homeowners
love it for low-maintenance **kitchen countertops**.

### **4) Can I replace countertops without fully remodeling my kitchen?**

Yes. Many homeowners upgrade countertops as a standalone project and see a
huge improvement immediately—especially when paired with a new sink or
backsplash.

### **5) How do I choose the right countertop color for my kitchen?**

We suggest starting with your cabinet color and lighting. In many Pompano
Beach homes, bright whites and soft veining work beautifully for a clean,
coastal look.
