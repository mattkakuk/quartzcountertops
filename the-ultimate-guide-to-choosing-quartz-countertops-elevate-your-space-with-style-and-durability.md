## **Understanding Quartz Countertops: What Are They?**

### **What Is Quartz?**

Quartz is a mineral found naturally in the Earth’s crust. When engineered into
countertops, it combines natural quartz with resins and pigments, creating a
material that boasts both strength and beauty.

### **How Are Quartz Countertops Made?**

The manufacturing process involves crushing quartz into small pieces and then
blending it with polymers, dyes, and other bonding agents. This mixture is
then molded into slabs, polished, and finished to perfect the surface.

### **Is Quartz a Natural or Engineered Material?**

While quartz is naturally occurring, the countertops we see today are
engineered products, designed to maximize durability and aesthetic appeal.
